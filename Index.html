About Our School

Excellence in education since 2001

Our Mission

At Korley Regional Senior High School, we are committed to providing a nurturing and challenging environment where every child can reach their full potential. We foster creativity, critical thinking, and character development in our students.

Our Vision

We aim be the best leading Senior High school that prepares students to be responsible, confident, critical thinkers and successful individuals who contribute positively to society revolutiong and inovations in all aspect of development.
